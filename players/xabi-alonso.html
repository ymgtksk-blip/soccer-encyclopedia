Xabi Alonso

Spanish former midfielder and deep-lying playmaker for Real Sociedad, Liverpool, Real Madrid and Bayern Munich. Won the 2010 World Cup, Euro 2008 and 2012, and two Champions Leagues. 114 caps. Now a manager.
