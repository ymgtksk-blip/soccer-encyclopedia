Thomas Partey

Ghanaian defensive midfielder, born 1993. Plays for Villarreal after joining on a free transfer from Arsenal in 2025; formerly Atlético Madrid. Won the Europa League, UEFA Super Cup and La Liga; longtime Ghana international and vice-captain.
