Jordan Ayew

𝕏 Share
Ghanaian forward born in Marseille, France in 1991, son of Abedi Pelé and brother of André Ayew. Ghana captain with 123 caps and 34 goals; played for Marseille, Lorient, Aston Villa, Swansea, Crystal Palace and Leicester City, released in 2026.
