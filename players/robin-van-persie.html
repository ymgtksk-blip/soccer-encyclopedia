Robin van Persie

Dutch striker who scored 50 goals in 102 caps and was long the Netherlands' record scorer until Memphis Depay surpassed him in 2025. Won the Premier League with Manchester United and the FA Cup with Arsenal; now retired, he has worked as a manager including at Feyenoord.
