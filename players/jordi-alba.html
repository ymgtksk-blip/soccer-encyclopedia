Jordi Alba

𝕏 Share
Jordi Alba is a Spanish left-back who spent the bulk of his career at Barcelona winning six La Liga titles and the 2014-15 Champions League, won Euro 2012 with Spain, and retired after playing for Inter Miami at the end of the 2025 season.
