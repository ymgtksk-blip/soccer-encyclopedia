Michael Ballack

𝕏 Share
Michael Ballack is a retired German box-to-box central midfielder who captained the national team and won league and cup honours with Bayern Munich and Chelsea, but famously finished runner-up in the 2002 World Cup, Euro 2008, and two Champions League finals.
