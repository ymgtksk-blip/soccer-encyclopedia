Keisuke Honda

𝕏 Share
Keisuke Honda is a Japanese left-footed attacking midfielder who starred for CSKA Moscow and AC Milan, earned 98 caps (37 goals) for Japan across three World Cups, and in 2026 signed to continue his career with Singapore Premier League club Albirex Jurong (the rebranded Albirex Niigata Singapore).
